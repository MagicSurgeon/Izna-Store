Your Order Journey

Order Placement

When you place an order, you’ll select your products, provide your shipping and payment information, and complete your purchase. You’ll receive a confirmation email with your order details and reference number.

Order Processing

We verify your order, check payment and stock availability, and prepare your items for packing. This ensures everything is in order before we get your package ready.

Packing and Handling

Your items are carefully packed with protective materials to ensure they arrive safely. We label the package and prepare it for shipment.

Shipping and Dispatch

Your package is handed over to our shipping carrier, and you’ll receive a tracking number. This lets you monitor your order’s journey to your doorstep.

In-Transit Monitoring

We track your package while it’s on the way to you. If there are any issues or delays, we address them promptly and keep you updated on its status.

Delivery and Confirmation

Your package arrives at your address, and we confirm that it’s been delivered. We may follow up to ensure you’re satisfied with your order.
